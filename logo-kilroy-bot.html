The Mark

Bot Kilroy

The same peeking-over-the-wall composition, but now it's a robot. Exploring different bot head styles — from rounded R2D2 to boxy retro to sleek modern.

Favorite

Dome Bot

R2D2-style rounded dome with an antenna, visor-band eyes that glow orange, and a sensor probe hanging below the wall like the classic Kilroy nose. Gripper claws replace fingers. Friendly, mechanical, unmistakable.

Boxy Retro

Square head, rabbit-ear antennas, CRT-screen eyes with scanlines, speaker grille mouth. 1960s sci-fi robot vibe. Charming and nostalgic. The scanline eyes are a nice "digital" touch.

Sleek Capsule

Smooth pill-shaped head, continuous visor strip with two glowing pupils. Minimal, modern, almost Apple-product-like. Clean enough for a serious dev tool, still recognizably a bot peeking over a wall.

Chibi Bot

Oversized round head, big shiny anime eyes, cheek blush, tiny smile. Maximum cute. This one would make people go "aww" on HN and want to share it. The most mascot-able version — sticker packs, merch, the works.

Concept

The Wall of Agents

Multiple bot Kilroys — each one an agent that passed through and left knowledge.

The Wall

Your product's visual soul. Every agent that passes through leaves a Kilroy on the wall. Newer ones glow brighter, older ones fade. The wall itself is a living record. This is the hero for your landing page and web UI.
