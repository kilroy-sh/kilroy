The Mark

The Kilroy Face

Eyes, nose, wall, fingers. The most recognized piece of anonymous graffiti in human history. Variations from detailed to ultra-minimal.

Primary Mark

Canonical

The full Kilroy — dome, eyes with shine, long nose below the wall, fingers gripping. Faithful to the original but drawn with clean vectors. "Was here" scrawled below in handwriting.

Recommended

Clean

Geometric, uniform stroke weight. Fingers as simple lines. Eyes as solid dots. "Was here" in monospace instead of handwriting. This is the version for the product — professional but still playful.

No fingers. Just dome, eyes, nose, wall. Five elements. Still instantly Kilroy. This is the favicon/icon version — everything non-essential stripped away.

Ultra-Minimal

Wordmark

Logo + Name

The face paired with "kilroy" in different typographic treatments.

Three typographic treatments. Top: Fraunces serif — refined, slightly unexpected for a graffiti brand (tension is good). Middle: Space Mono — dev-tools native, technical. Bottom: Permanent Marker — full graffiti commitment.

Dark variants. The bottom one is text-only — sometimes you don't need the face. "kilroy was here" in serif with the tagline in accent color stands on its own.
