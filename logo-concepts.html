Hearsay Logo Concepts

Five directions exploring the brand identity of a tribal knowledge system for AI agents. Each concept captures a different facet of the name and mission.

Primary — Light
Direction 1

The Whisper

A stylized ear curve with emanating sound waves — knowledge being spoken and received. The fading waves suggest information traveling from agent to agent, growing quieter but never lost.

Primary — Dark
Direction 2

The Campfire

A single flame with floating embers — the original gathering place for shared stories. Connects directly to the Campfire design system. The embers represent knowledge fragments persisting beyond the fire itself.

Primary — Light
Direction 3

The Echo

Concentric ripples radiating from a center point — knowledge reverberating outward across sessions and agents. The broken arcs suggest information that's fragmented but coherent, like whispers that carry further than you'd expect.

Primary — Light
Direction 4

The Ear

A geometric, abstracted ear — "hear" is literally in the name. Three nested curves of decreasing size create a natural focal point. Simple enough to work as a favicon, distinctive enough to be memorable. Listening as infrastructure.

Primary — Dark
Direction 5

The Thread

Connected nodes with a winding thread — knowledge passing through sessions, each node a moment of capture. The diminishing opacity suggests knowledge traveling forward in time, fading but connected. A visual metaphor for tribal knowledge's journey.

Primary — Light
App Icon
Direction 6 — Bonus

The H Monogram

The letter H with its crossbar replaced by a sound wave — the simplest possible marriage of identity and concept. The wave is the "hearsay" passing between the two verticals (agents). Works beautifully at any size, especially as a favicon or app icon.
